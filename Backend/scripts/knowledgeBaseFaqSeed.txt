Q: How do I apply for a program transfer at SLIIT?
A: To apply for a program transfer for the 2026 January-June semester:
1. Obtain the Program Transfer Form from Student Services or download from SLIIT website / CourseWeb.
2. Fill in your current program, desired program, and reasons for transfer.
3. Attach supporting documents (academic transcripts, faculty recommendation if required).
4. Submit the form to the Student Services Division before the transfer deadline.
5. Transfers are subject to seat availability and management approval.
6. You will be notified of the decision via your SLIIT email.

Note: You must have completed at least one full semester to be eligible. Contact Student Services for specific eligibility criteria.

Q: How do I apply to sit for an examination that I missed due to illness or absence?
A: If you missed an examination due to illness or an emergency:
1. Obtain a valid medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner.
2. Submit the certificate to Student Services within 3 working days of the missed exam.
3. Fill out the Excuse for Absence / Medical Referral request form from the Student Services office.
4. Await approval from your Faculty Head or the Registrar's office.
5. You may be allowed to sit a replacement or repeat examination at the next available opportunity.

Note: Self-declared illness without medical documentation will not be accepted.
Contact Student Services for the current form (Excuse for Absence guidelines, version 3, updated 11.12.2025).

Q: What are the rules for SLIIT examinations that students must follow?
A: SLIIT Examination Rules (students must adhere strictly):
- Students must bring their valid Student ID card to every examination.
- Entry to the examination hall closes 30 minutes after the exam starts.
- No electronic devices (mobile phones, smartwatches, etc.) are allowed in the hall.
- Bags and personal belongings must be left outside. Only allowed stationery may be brought in.
- Written materials and notes are prohibited unless it is an open-book exam.
- Students must remain seated for the full duration unless permitted by the supervisor.
- Academic dishonesty or cheating will result in disciplinary action including possible dismissal.
- If you fall ill during an exam, inform the supervisor immediately.
- Results may be withheld if examination fees are not settled.

Q: What is the procedure if I find a suspicious package or receive a bomb threat on campus?
A: If you find a suspicious package:
- DO NOT handle, open, or move the object.
- Turn off nearby portable radios and cell phones.
- Move away from the area immediately and secure the area by closing nearby doors.
- Immediately inform CSSU at 011 754 3161 or Ext 3161, or dial 119 / Army Bomb Squad at 011 243 4251.

If you receive a bomb threat by phone:
- Remain calm and do not hang up.
- Note the caller's exact words, voice details, and any background sounds.
- Immediately inform CSSU after the call.

Q: What should I do if I discover a fire on campus?
A: If you discover a fire on campus, follow these steps:
1. Activate the fire alarm on your way out of the building.
2. If minor, use a fire extinguisher - remember PASS: Pull the pin, Aim at the base, Squeeze the handle, Sweep side to side.
3. Do NOT use elevators - use fire stairs only.
4. If smoke is present, stay near the floor where air is less toxic.
5. Close doors and windows as you leave if safe to do so.
6. Report to your designated assembly point after evacuating.
7. For serious fires, dial 119 and notify Campus Security (CSSU) at 011 754 3161 or Ext: 3161.
Do not re-enter an evacuated building until an authorized officer declares it safe.
